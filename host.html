Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as a Homeo host?

Earnings vary based on your location, property type, and how often you host. On average, hosts earn between $5,000-$10,000 per year. Our pricing tools can help you determine the optimal price for your space.

What are the requirements to become a host?

To become a Homeo host, you need to have a spare room, apartment, or property that meets our basic safety and cleanliness standards. You'll also need to verify your identity and provide necessary documentation based on local regulations.

How does Homeo protect my property?

We offer $1 million in property damage protection and $1 million in liability insurance for each booking. Our secure payment system ensures you get paid, and our verification process helps ensure guests are trustworthy.

Can I set my own house rules?

Yes, you have full control over your house rules. You can specify check-in/check-out times, whether pets are allowed, smoking policies, and any other rules important to you. These are clearly communicated to guests before they book.
