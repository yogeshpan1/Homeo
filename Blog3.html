Discovering Kathmandu's Best Restaurants

Kathmandu is not just a city of temples and history; it's a paradise for food lovers. From traditional Nepalese dishes to modern fusion cuisine, the city offers something for everyone. Here's a guide to some must-visit restaurants.

1. Bhojan Griha

A heritage restaurant set in a traditional Nepali house, Bhojan Griha offers authentic Newari cuisine in a cultural ambiance. Don't miss trying the momo, chatamari, and local desserts.

2. OR2K

Located in Thamel, OR2K is famous for its vegetarian and Middle Eastern-inspired menu. The relaxed, artsy environment makes it perfect for a casual meal with friends.

3. Fire and Ice Pizzeria

For pizza lovers, Fire and Ice serves some of the best wood-fired pizzas in Kathmandu. Their cozy outdoor seating is ideal for enjoying a meal on a warm evening.

4. The Old House

Combining international and local flavors, The Old House is known for its warm hospitality and beautifully plated dishes. Try their Nepalese thali for a complete cultural experience.

5. Roadhouse Cafe

A trendy spot for breakfast, brunch, and coffee, Roadhouse Cafe is popular among locals and tourists alike. Their pancakes, sandwiches, and specialty coffees are highly recommended.

Kathmandu's restaurant scene is vibrant and constantly evolving. Exploring these spots gives visitors a taste of the city's culinary diversity, whether you're looking for traditional dishes or modern flavors.
