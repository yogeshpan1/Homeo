About the home

Discover a sanctuary of peace and unparalleled beauty at The Himalayan Heaven. Nestled in the heart of Nepal's majestic landscape, our boutique hotel offers breathtaking, panoramic views of the world's most iconic peaks. We blend authentic Nepali hospitality with modern comfort, providing a serene base for your adventures or a peaceful retreat for your soul. Your journey to paradise begins here.

Perched on a tranquil hillside, The Himalayan Heaven is more than just a hotel—it is an experience. Wake up to the golden sun illuminating the snow-capped crowns of the Annapurna range and fall asleep under a blanket of the clearest stars you've ever seen.

We are dedicated to providing a haven of comfort and tranquility. Each of our rooms and suites is thoughtfully designed with warm, traditional Nepali decor complemented by all the modern amenities you need. Drift into blissful sleep in our luxurious beds, and step out onto your private balcony to sip a warm drink while the mountains whisper their ancient stories.
